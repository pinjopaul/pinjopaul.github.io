1. Discovery Sprint (1-2 Week Diagnostics)

The initial diagnostic phase focuses on rapid prioritization of high-potential opportunities rather than exhaustive brainstorming.

Data Readiness

Organizations assess their "AI readiness," focusing on data quality and the elimination of redundant, obsolete, and trivial (ROT) information.

Opportunity Mapping

Teams identify 3-5 key challenges or postponed opportunities where AI can move the needle positively.

Risk Review

Automated tools identify potential security gaps and compliance risks (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) early in the process.

2. Pilot Launch (Measurable Slice)

Modern pilots move from experimentation to "execution at pace," deploying a focused slice of the solution with clear 90-day proof-of-value gates.

KPI Tracking

Pilots measure specific metrics such as Deflection Rates (40-50% target for inquiries), First-Contact Resolution, and Response Time reductions.

Hardening

Security and governance are baked in from day one, using role-based access and compliance monitoring to transition from "utility pilots" to production-ready systems.

Feedback Loops

Real-time development analytics track cycle time and quality, allowing teams to adjust before process bottlenecks impact broader delivery.

Continuity
